The Tor Project

The Tor Project, Inc. is a Massachusetts-based 501 research-education nonprofit organization founded by computer scientists Roger Dingledine, Nick Mathewson and five others. The Tor Project is primarily responsible for maintaining software for the Tor anonymity network.
